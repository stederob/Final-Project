Melon Service by Josef Hoffmann

The "Melon Service" of Josef Hoffmann is a tea service for two people. The service is based on a simple curves replicated so as to perceive greater roundness. The simplicity of the curves has allowed me to develop the whole service The "Melon Service" consists of a jug, a teapot, a sugar bowl and two cups.

CARAFE

The carafe is given by most industry verticals of different heights.

TEAPOT

The teapot is made ​​up of several concentric curves that give the different depths.

SUGAR BOWL

The sugar is almost spherical if it were not for the sector occupied by the cover.

CUP

The cups show their sophistication in saucers with petal-shaped sectors.

This is the final result.
